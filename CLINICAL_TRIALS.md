# Clinical Trials — Affordable Prosthetic Solutions

## Identified Trials

### 1. ReMotion Knee — Reverse Innovation (NCT04700085)
- **Status:** COMPLETED
- **Sponsor:** Sint Maartenskliniek
- **Focus:** $80 mechanical prosthetic knee for transfemoral amputees
- **Key Findings:**
  - ReMotion Knee costs ~$80 vs. $500-$5000 for standard mechanical knees
  - Tested in high-income country (Netherlands) as reverse innovation
  - ISO 10328 certified, CE Mark received
  - Polycentric design, 3-5 year lifespan
  - No hydraulic/pneumatic system — simpler, cheaper
  - Functional outcomes slightly lower than high-end knees but significantly more affordable
- **URL:** https://clinicaltrials.gov/study/NCT04700085

### 2. Low-Cost Prosthetic Foot Biomechanical Study (NCT06390033)
- **Status:** COMPLETED
- **Sponsor:** Hong Kong Polytechnic University
- **Focus:** Low-cost prosthetic foot vs. K2-K3 standard for transtibial amputees
- **Key Findings:**
  - Compared low-cost prosthetic foot to traditional K2-K3 feet
  - Evaluated gait mechanics, balance, and physical performance
  - Indoor and outdoor activity assessment
  - Berg Balance Test, Four Square Step Test, 10-Meter Walk Test
  - User satisfaction comparison
- **URL:** https://clinicaltrials.gov/study/NCT06390033
