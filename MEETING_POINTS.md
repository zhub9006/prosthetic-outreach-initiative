## Recommended Meeting Locations

### Option 1: Lexington, KY ⭐ (Recommended — Equal Access)
- **Drive times**: Chicago ~5hrs, Boston ~6.5hrs, Atlanta ~6.3hrs
- **Why**: Most equitable drive times from all three regions; well-connected via I-64/I-75/I-71
- **Venue area**: Avenue of Champions (near University of Kentucky)
- **Pros within 2km of midpoint (38.20, -84.50)**:
  - Multiple coffee shops, casual restaurants, private dining rooms suitable for workshops
  - University facilities available (UK campus venues)
  - Affordable lodging options along Nicholasville Road / Broadway
  - Central to all three regions when factoring plane + drive or direct drive

### Option 2: Pittsburgh, PA (Major City, Excellent Venues)
- **Drive times**: Chicago ~4.5hrs, Boston ~5hrs, Atlanta ~7hrs
- **Why**: Major city with extensive hotel/conference venue options; airport with direct flights from all three cities
- **Amenities**: Multiple conference facilities, hotels, co-working spaces, university venues (CMU, Pitt)
- **Pros**: Best for hybrid (in-person + virtual) meetings; good food & accommodation

### Option 3: Hagerstown, MD (Geographic Route Hub)
- **Drive times**: ~7hrs from each city
- **Why**: I-70/I-81 junction; central location on East Coast
- **Pros**: Good meeting facilities, hotels, lower cost than major metros

### Option 4: Charlotte, NC (Southeast-Lean)
- **Drive times**: Atlanta ~4hrs, Chicago ~7hrs, Boston ~8hrs
- **Why**: Growing co-working and meeting space scene; major airport hub
- **Pros**: Best if meeting leans toward Southeast coordination

### Option 5: Virtual / Hybrid ⭐ (Best for Regular Check-Ins)
- Consider rotating in-person meetings with virtual participation
- Reduces travel burden on any single team
- Use quarterly or bi-annual in-person + monthly virtual

## Meeting Logistics Guidelines

- Rotate meeting locations across regions for fairness
- Book venues 4–6 weeks in advance
- Share travel cost estimates with all regional teams
- Maintain virtual option for team members unable to travel
- Aim for wheelchair-accessible, transit-friendly venues
- Coordinate group lodging near venue to reduce costs
