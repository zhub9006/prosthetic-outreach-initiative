## Recommended Meeting Locations

### Option 1: Hagerstown, MD
- Central to all three cities (driving distance ~7-8 hours from each)
- Good highway access (I-70, I-81)
- Multiple conference facilities and cafes

### Option 2: Pittsburgh, PA
- Accessible from Chicago (~4.5 hrs), Boston (~5 hrs), Atlanta (~7 hrs)
- Major city with extensive venue options
- University facilities available

### Option 3: Charlotte, NC
- Accessible from Atlanta (~4 hrs), Chicago (~7 hrs), Boston (~8 hrs)
- Growing coworking and meeting space scene

### Option 4: Virtual / Hybrid
- Consider rotating in-person meetings with virtual participation
- Reduces travel burden on any single team

## Guidelines
- Rotate meeting locations across regions for fairness
- Book venues 4-6 weeks in advance
- Share travel cost estimates with all regional teams
