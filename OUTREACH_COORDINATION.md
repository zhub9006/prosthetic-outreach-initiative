# Outreach Coordination Hub

This document coordinates volunteer task management across Chicago, Boston, and Atlanta teams for the prosthetic outreach initiative.

---

## Task Categories

| Category | Description | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| Clinical Trials | Identify & analyze accessible prosthetic trials | Research Team |
| Low-Cost Solutions | Evaluate affordable prosthetic options under $500 | Research Team |
| Community Partnerships | Connect with DME vendors, nonprofits, and clinics | Partnerships Team |
| Regional Outreach | Organize local events and volunteer recruitment | Regional Teams |
| Volunteer Scheduling | Coordinate cross-regional meeting schedules | Coordination Team |

## Regional Teams

- **Leader**: Boston-based prosthetics research hub

### Atlanta (Southeast)
- **Leader**: Atlanta-based DME and community outreach coordinators
- **Focus**: Underserved population access, rural clinic partnerships, collaborative care networks
- **Key Trials**: Active trials at Emory, Shepherd Center, Georgia Tech

## Meeting Point Analysis: Lexington, KY

Lexington is the recommended cross-regional meeting location. It is the geographic midpoint among Chicago, Boston, and Atlanta.

| Route | Distance | Drive Time |
|---|---|---|
| Chicago → Lexington | ~801 km (498 mi) | ~5 hrs |
| Boston → Lexington | ~1079 km (670 mi) | ~6.5 hrs |
| Atlanta → Lexington | ~980 km (610 mi) | ~6.3 hrs |

All teams can reach Lexington within **7 hours of driving**, making it the most equitable location for quarterly in-person meetings.

### Recommended Venue Area: Avenue of Champions, Lexington
Restaurants and meeting-friendly venues near the midpoint:
- **Qdoba Mexican Grill** – 265 Avenue of Champions, open 7am–11pm daily, wheelchair-accessible
- **Papa John's** – 265 Avenue of Champions, open 10am–midnight, wheelchair-accessible
- **Apothecary Cafe** – College of Pharmacy Building, open Mon-Fri 7am+, affordable coffee
- **Donut Days Bakery** – 185 Southland Drive, coffee & pastries
- **Smashing Tomato** – 2573 Richmond Road (pizza restaurant with online ordering)

### Venue Recommendations
1. **Sweet Cane Cafe** – Near UK campus, private dining room available
2. **Arbutus Brewing** – Communal seating, relaxed environment for team workshops
3. **Compass Coffee** – Multiple locations, wheelchair-accessible, good for small group meetings

## Priority Labels for GitHub Issues

- `high-priority` — Urgent tasks requiring immediate action
- `clinical-trials` — Clinical trial research and analysis
- `research` — General research tasks
- `volunteer` — Volunteer coordination and scheduling
- `regional-chicago` / `regional-boston` / `regional-atlanta` — City-specific work
- `partnerships` — Partner development and outreach
- `community` — Community engagement and event planning
- `low-cost-solutions` — Affordable prosthetic research tasks

---

## Getting Started

1. Browse `high-priority` labeled issues to claim urgent tasks
2. Add your username as assignee to claimed issues
3. Apply regional labels (`regional-chicago`, `regional-boston`, `regional-atlanta`)
4. Track progress by updating issue status
5. Coordinate cross-regional meetings via the quarterly sync schedule
