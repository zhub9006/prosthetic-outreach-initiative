# Contributing to Prosthetic Outreach

## Teams
- **Research Team**: Identifies clinical trials and low-cost prosthetic solutions
- **Outreach Teams** (per region): Local outreach planning and volunteer coordination
- **Partnerships Team**: Establishes provider and nonprofit partnerships

## Getting Started
1. Fork/update the project repository
2. Claim an issue by adding your username as assignee
3. Add regional labels to your work
4. Update issue status as you progress

## Labels
- `clinical-trials` — clinical trial research
- `research` — general research tasks
- `volunteer` — volunteer coordination
- `regional` + `{city}` — city-specific work
- `high-priority` — urgent tasks
- `partnerships` — partner development
- `community` — community engagement
